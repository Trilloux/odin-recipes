Cyberpunk Cheeseburger

A cheeseburger is a classic American dish, featuring a ground beef patty that's typically grilled or fried, served inside a soft hamburger bun. What makes it distinct is the addition of a slice of melted cheese, often cheddar or American, atop the hot patty. Common toppings include lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, ketchup, and mustard.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Onion Slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the patties
  2. Preheat grill or pan
  3. Cook patties
  4. Add cheese
  5. Toast the buns
  6. Prepare toppings
  7. Assambe the Cheeseburger
  8. Serve immediately
