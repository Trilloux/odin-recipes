Cyberpunk Carbonara

Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish characterized by a creamy sauce made with eggs, cheese (usually Pecorino Romano), guanciale or pancetta (cured pork), and black pepper. The sauce is typically mixed with hot pasta, creating a rich and flavorful combination.

Ingredients

  • Sphaggetti
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Roman cheese
  • guanciale or pancetta
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Prepare the sauce ingredients
  3. Cook the pork
  4. Drain the Pasta
  5. Combine ingredients
  6. Add the sauce
  7. Adjust consistency
  8. Season with pepper
  9. Serve immediately
